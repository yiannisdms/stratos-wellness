The Stratos Way

We don't fix bodies.
We give them room
to remember themselves.

Modern life compresses the body — into screens, into commutes, into deadlines. Stratos is the deliberate counterweight. Our therapists train for years before they ever touch a client, and then keep training. Restraint, attention, and respect for the nervous system are the only tools we never put down.
